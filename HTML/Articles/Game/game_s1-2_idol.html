Idol Clicker

9 July 2021

And idle clicker (who'd have guessed)

Collect precious resources and magical idols in this punny idol game. Idols adore you click and will grant you with many blessings. But be warned: some will demand a sacrifice in return.

Idol clicker is the first full game I produced. It's not the most balanced but still and enjoyable play with varied idol types and upgrades. I was especially pleased with how idol upgrades offered interesting trade-offs in the context of an idol game. (Although, one may argue that this goes against the genre.)

Have fun collecting manna, hording gold and grabbing valuable rubies!
