What, when and why will I be doing. For the first time so far, I have decided to plan ahead. I must submit in two weeks, and I should be ready to ensure that I don’t have to rush to get that done.

What must I submit?

I have not clue. The brief isn’t out yet. But I presume that it’ll be an improvement of my current site. Given my insistence on another reboot and the need to juggle other projects too, I can’t really afford to site and wait for the next brief before devising a scheme and breaking ground.

I plan to have v0.3 (this iteration) ready, with all the functionalities of the previous site, by midnight on Wednesday (15 June). That will give me a week and a half to add new features. I also want to setup a better method for code display by then because I am doing a lot of that now and the current system is award to use and ugly to view.

This sounds over ambitious…

I am always over ambitious. Doing the work of two moths in two days sounds crazy – but the through has already been done. I have no need to faf or be distracted by design. I just need to implement and rework. My hope is that a strict schedule will force me to focus on the task at hand.
