Cat-a-pult

7 May 2021

A cat-launching micro-build

Cat-a-pult was a project where I investigated implementing a catapult with physics based projectile lunching. It is also one of the first unity project which I would consider to be playable.

I feel I have come a long way since Cat-a-pult: in terms of game design and programming. It's still definitely worth a play - althought it's far from purrfect. The level design is quite interesting and requires a bit of though. I remember being so proud of how I rendered the elastic and controlled the camera. It's nice to look back because it reminds one how they have grown and what they still must learn.

Now go have the time of you (9) lives shooting cats and catching rats!
