Shuffle

26 June 2022

A sliding block fidget puzzle

Don’t be fooled by the simple controls and intuitive rules, shuffle is deceptively complex.

Designed to encourage puzzle solving through paly, shuffle is a sliding block game where all block slide together. This puts a slightly different spin(or tilt) on the traditional sliding block puzzle. Block’s can be supports as well as blocked. One moment and asset the next and annoyance.

While not perfect, Help Me Out is one of my most developed projects to date and one I had great fun making! I would love your feedback on it because it might just develop it further…

Do you have what it takes to shuffle your way through 21 treacherous levels?
