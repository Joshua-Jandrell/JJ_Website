Star Dust

19 September 2021

A resource management game about space and time

Your star is dying, and time is running out! The fate of the species is in your hands! Send probes to distant planets to harvest the resources you need to upgrade you space station. You must enter interstellar space before it’s too late.

Star Dust was an attempt to create a resource management simulator where distance and time are themselves precious resources. This protype is rather unbalanced and the UI can be confusing at time. It’s still and interesting concept worth sharing.
